The History

Latest Vidz is a family business that was started by the Mbatha family who built the business and still run the business today.
Latest Vidz was started by the Mbatha family in 2015 as a way to provide the local people with the latest movies and video games in both industries.
The owners of Latest Vidz, which is the Mbatha family,
are very passionate about both these industries and they aim to provide local people with the best from both industries that come from both, South Africa and other countries.
In 2017, Latest Vidz partnered up with a few local movie studios to promote movies that are Proudly made in South Africa.
Also in 2017, after partnering up with the local movie studio, Latest Vidz has had a few events that raise funds for Mr Kay's Children Welfare.

The Mission

Latest Vidz' mission is to provide the local people with local movies and
movies from around the world, from Bollywood to Hollywood and
also video games from small indie studios to triple A titles.

The Vision

Latest Vidz' vision is to be able to have a stronger online presence online.
Latest Vidz would like to be able to provide our services online and
so that Latest Vidz can be able to engage with more customers online.
