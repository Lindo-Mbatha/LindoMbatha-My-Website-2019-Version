Our Work

Latest Vidz is a business that rents out Movies and rents out and sells Video Games
But that is not all Latest Vidz does.

Latest Vidz has partnered up with a few local movie studios to promote local talent.
An event is organized to showcase all the local movies made and all sales on that day are given to the team that with involved in the making of the movie.
The local movie studios:
Sasani Studios (Johannesburg)
Cape Town Film Studios (Cape Town)
KwaZulu Studios (Durban)

Latest Vidz has partnered up with a few international studios to promote their movies.
60% of the amount from the promotions Latest Vidz does are all given to charities in the local area and around South Africa.
The international studios:
Warner Bros.
Sony Pictures Motion Picture Group
Walt Disney Studios
Universal Pictures
20th Century Fox
Paramount Pictures
Lionsgate Films

Latest Vidz has partnered with Mr Kay's Children Welfare.
The relationship between the two companies is great and it's a long term partnership
Latest Vidz funds Mr Kay's Children Welfare with all the sales from the events held monthly to support the children.
